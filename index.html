Our Operational Process

Harnessing nature's strength through precision conditioning—engineered to elevate your roots.

Washing & Buffering

Carefully washed multiple times in fresh water to reduce salt content and achieve Low EC.

Sun Drying

Natural drying on pure concrete floors to ensure optimal moisture levels before compression.

Hydraulic Pressing

Compressed using high-quality hydraulic presses into standardized export-ready blocks.

Quality Assurance

Each batch undergoes rigorous laboratory testing before packaging and global dispatch.
